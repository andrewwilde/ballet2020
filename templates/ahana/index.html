What kind of a Dance Studio are we?

We are a non-competitive dance studio that offers dance classes in ballet, tap, jazz, contemporary, hip hop, and tumbling. We adhere to our mission statement in all we do!

Caring Teachers

Our teachers are experienced and strive to help each student feel welcome to each dance class. Using patience, kindness, creativity and perserverance, children learn the proper techniques in a way that will foster a love for dance.

Comfortable Environment

We've upgraded our studio to allow our students more room to dance! Parents can watch their children through a one-way window that allows students to dance with fewer distractions. We're non-competitive, so children can come and dance in a low-stress environment.

Outstanding Value

By keeping our prices low, we offer an affordable option for families with tight budgets.

Mission Statement

We are so excited to share our love and passion for dance with everyone. We will strive to create a comfortable environment where students can learn new dance skills, or grow skills they've already developed. Our wonderful teachers will foster an increased love for ballet, tap, jazz, contemporary, and hip hop using a fun and engaging curriculum. Everyone will feel welcome in a relaxed, structured environment. We value the input of students and parents, and we will do everything possible to promote an amazing experience for our students.

Petit Ballet Academy would be honored to have you join us in our program!
